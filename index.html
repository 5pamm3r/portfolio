How much does it cost to get started with a high-performing website?

It will depend upon the scope and requirements of the project. For reference, past projects have ranged between $3,000 - $10,000.

On the discovery call, we will seek to identify specifics and objectives that can affect the cost. These are things like number of pages, specific features, timeline, etc. All these will contribute to the cost and will vary from project to project.

If all you need is a basic, one-pager landing page, then this will cost you around $2,000-$4,000.

How long will the work take from start to finish?

It will depend on the scope of the project.

  • A full website with 5-10 pages may take 6-8 weeks.
